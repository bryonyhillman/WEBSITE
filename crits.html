How to Apply

To take part, please email me with:

  • Images of the work you’d like to discuss
  • A short description of your practice
  • A brief note on what you’d like to get out of the session

Selection

Groups are put together with a mix of practices and perspectives in mind, so the conversation stays active and useful.

Each session is limited to six people to make sure there’s enough time for each artist. You will be notified by email if you have been selected for a session.

Staying in touch

If you’re interested in taking part in future sessions, feel free to email even if you can’t make the upcoming one. The aim is to build an ongoing group of artists in dialogue with each other and their work - something that can continue to develop over time rather than sit as a one-off.

INFO

These sessions are run as a part of The Horses Mouth Programme, an artist-led crit session run by The Bomb Factory Art Foundation built around sharing work, group discussion, and shared feedback in an informal setting. Sessions are free to attend.

Each session brings together six artists to share work and spend time in structured conversation. The aim is to get a clearer sense of how the work is coming across, while also opening up wider questions around your practice.
